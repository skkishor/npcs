Bed Bugs

Bed Bugs thrive at room temperature and with an suitable food supply, females can lay up to 500 eggs in a lifetime and they can possibly live for more than 300 days. Bed Bugs feed on blood so bites are common, people will have different reactions to them, minimum they can be very itchy.

You can pick up bed bugs almost anywhere — offices, stores, hotels and gyms for starters. They’re great at hiding and have been known to hitchhike in luggage, personal belongings or even you. Once indoors, they can be very difficult to control without the help of an experienced pest specialist. You can reduce your chances of a costly bed bug infestation by catching them early. NPCS will customize a treatment to quickly and safely remove bed bugs and their eggs from all infested rooms or areas. If bed bugs are found in the treated room or area within the next 30 days, NPCS will perform further treatment at no additional cost.
