Bees

There are over 20,000 recorded bee species in the world. While some bees are solitary others such as the honey bee live in hives with their colony. Bees tend to swarm when they are large in population or with seasonal changes.

The queen bee lays the eggs while the drones fertilize her, worker bees gather pollen may only live six weeks. Disease and starvation or an ailing queen may result in the loss of the colony.
