Fleas

Most people hate the thought of fleas in the house, pets can pick up fleas very easily by moving through grass or rolling around on the ground. Fleas love hairy animals like long haired cats or dogs. Fleas can jump long distances and bite their host to feed on their blood. Fleas are really small and black colored.
